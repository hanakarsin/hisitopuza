Cindy Kimberly - Age, Family, Bio

Instagram sensation who gained fame after Justin Bieber posted a photo of her on his feed and asked "Omg who is this!!!" She went on to become a model, appearing on the cover of GQ Spain in 2018 and Sports Illustrated in 2022.

Before Fame

She became an overnight celebrity in 2015 while employed as a babysitter. In 2016, she began to model.

Trivia

She posts portraits of herself that her fans draw of her and send to her. Her wolfiecindy Instagram account has more than 7 million followers.

Family Life

She is from Costa Blanca, Spain. She had a long-term relationship with model Neels Visser. In 2022, she began dating footballer Dele Alli.

Associated With

She and Yasmim Senna both became social media stars thanks in part to a connection with Justin Bieber.
