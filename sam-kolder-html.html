Sam Kolder - Age, Family, Bio

Filmmaker, editor and photographer who was hired to be the head of video of Beautiful Destinations. The Beautiful Destinations Instagram account had more than 7 million followers as of September of 2016, while he has earned more than 1.6 million followers on his own personal account.

Before Fame

He was uploading video content to his "kold" YouTube channel as early as May of 2011.

Trivia

His YouTube channel has earned more than 1.3 million subscribers. He enjoys extreme sports like skateboarding and cliff jumping.

Family Life

He is from Toronto, Canada and would move to Los Angeles, California. His brother Tim passed away after a storm capsized his canoe in Algonquin Park. He began dating singer Arianna Hicks; however they later broke up in late 2017. He later began dating fellow Instagram star Chelsea Yamase. He has since been romantically linked to a creative director named Kai. 

Associated With

He shot video for The Chainsmokers, a duo comprised of Alex Pall and Andrew Taggart.
