I Am Ready To Have A Baby

Former BBNaija winner Mercy Eke has stated that she is ready to have a child.

This is coming after the news of Rihanna and A$AP Rocky pregnancy.

Mercy announced her thoughts to her fans in a post made on Instagram.

She said that her ovaries are ready and she wanted to experience the joys of motherhood. However, Eke maintained that she would wait for the right time before embarking on such a journey.

She wrote: “I feel like crying. I really wanna experience this at the right time.”

You know I’m so ready, my eggs are doing Tinini plus having you as my baby’s godmother chineke oge erugo”,

Earlier this year, the reality TV star had disclosed that she dreamt that she was winning souls for God and healing people.

She said this on her Snapchat story.

Eke further stated that she neither lied nor drank alcohol on the eighth day of the year, as she asked God for more strength.

The 28-year-old added that she wants to be different this year.

She wrote, “Day 8: No alcohol, no lies, no sin, no party. God, I need more strength to keep going but outside is already calling my name.

“I want to be different this year. I had a dream where I was winning souls for God(I was preaching and healing people).”
