Lauren Bennett - Age, Family, Bio

British singer and model who is known for being involved with many artists, including the girl groups G.R.L. and Paradiso Girls. 

Before Fame

She started out as part of the Paradiso Girls in 2007, and they had a debut single called "Patron Tequila" featuring Lil Jon and Eve. 

Trivia

She was featured in a 2010 remix of will.i.am's song "I Got it from My Mama." 

Family Life

She was born in Meopham, Kent, United Kingdom. She got engaged to dancer Kenny Wormald in June 2019. Their son Harlow Bennett was born on September 6, 2019.

Associated With

She was featured on Cee Lo Green's album The Lady Killer and his track "Love Gun" in 2010.
