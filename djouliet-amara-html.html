Djouliet Amara - Age, Family, Bio

Actress and dancer Instagram that has earned over 10,000 followers. Her breakout roles included a guest star role on Riverdale, a recurring role in the show Superman & Lois, and as of 2022 was in post-production for a starring role in Apple+ show The Big Door Prize. 

Before Fame

While born in Russia, her family immigrated to Winnipeg, Canada when she was very young. She started as a dancer and moved to New York City to pursue this goal before she pivoted to include modeling and acting to her resume. 

Trivia

She has limited her social media presence to only include her Instagram account. She speaks both Russian and English.

Family Life

She has pet parrot named Louie. Her mother is Russian and her father is from Sierra Leon, and this was the impetus for their leaving Russia as they wanted somewhere less racist to raise their daughter. 

Associated With

In 2022 she began filming a coming-of-age tale Bloody Hell and in her June instagram posts from that year she showed how much fun she is having with it's main star Maddie Ziegler.
