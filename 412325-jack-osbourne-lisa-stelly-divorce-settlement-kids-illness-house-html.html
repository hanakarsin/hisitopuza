What disease does Jack Osbourne have?

The reality TV star was working away from home when he suddenly lost vision in his right eye. After a period of 48 hours, he became partially blind. Before the eye symptoms occurred, he had been experiencing pinching and numbness in his leg for three months straight.

Osbourne made an appointment with the doctor, and he conducted multiple tests on him, including MRI. The tests revealed that he had lesions in his brain and spine. These were signs and symptoms of relapsing-remitting multiple sclerosis, a degenerative, neurologic condition. At the time of diagnosis, Jack was 26 years old.

He was placed on medication. Since he started his medication, his vision returned slowly. Soon after learning he had the condition, he tried to connect with anyone he knew with the disease to get firsthand, personal accounts of living with it. At the moment, he is committed to spreading awareness, sharing information, and connecting with others living with the condition.
