American rapper Cardi B thrilled by Kenyan matatu with her graffiti portraits

The Kenyan matatu industry is one that goes beyond basic transport of passengers between estates and the central business district.

It has embedded a mark on the global front for its over-the-bar public service entertainment, among them giant LCD screens, booming onboard music and graffiti.

The latest buzz in town is one that has images of American rapper Cardi B drawn on the side, front and rear.

Photos of the matatu caught the artist's attention, who took to social media to appreciate the individuals behind the craft.
