Christy O, Cyph Becomes First Housemates To Be Evicted

The Big Brother Naija season 7 show, which kicked off on July 23, had its first eviction of housemates on Sunday, 7th August 2022.

Christy O and Cyph were evicted from the show and it came as a surprise to some fans.

The eviction was announced by the show host, Ebuka Obi-Uchendu, during the BBNaija ‘Level Up’ Live show.

Christy O and Cyph were sent packing from the ‘Level Up’ house after garnering the lowest votes for the week.

Nini had been nominated for eviction alongside Amaka, Phyna, Christy O, Cyph, and Khalid.

During his interview on the stage, Cyph said Big Brother Naija has given him a platform and he’s ready to leverage on that outside the house.

However, Christy O was unable to make it to the stage.
