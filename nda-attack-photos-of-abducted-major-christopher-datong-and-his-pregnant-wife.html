Photos Of Abducted Major Christopher Datong And His Pregnant Wife

The photo of a military officer identified as Major Christopher Datong and his pregnant wife, who was abducted on Tuesday when bandit terrorists attacked the Nigerian Defence Academy (NDA) has surfaced online.

Naija News earlier reported that Major Christopher Datong, was amongst uniformed men of the NDA that were abducted on Tuesday when bandit terrorists attacked the Nigerian Defence Academy (NDA) in Kaduna.

There had been widespread reports that the lifeless body of the Major was discovered on Tuesday evening with insinuations that the terrorists had killed him but fresh reports indicate the officer is still alive.

Sharing a photo of the abducted military officer, a Facebook User, identified as Chidi Cali wrote;

‘This is Major Datong kidnapped at NDA by Fulani Militia, while Flight Lieutenant Okoronkwo was killed.’

Meanwhile, the Chief of Defence Staff (CDS), General Lucky Irabor has condemned the attack by gunmen on the Nigerian Defence Academy (NDA) in Kaduna, liking the incident to armed robbery in someone’s residence.

Naija News had reported that the attack, which occurred in the early hours of Tuesday, resulted in the deaths of two officers and the kidnap of another.

Addressing the incident while speaking with newsmen in Yola, the Adamawa capital, Irabor expressed concern over attacks by bandits, but assured that security agencies are on top of the situation.
