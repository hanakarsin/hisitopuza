Yung Chip - Age, Family, Bio

Popular Fortnite YouTuber named Josh who formerly shared FIFA videos. He has more than 2 million subscribers. He has also hosted The Fellas podcast with Calfreezy.

Before Fame

He joined the YouTube community on October 21, 2012. His channel was formerly called TheBurntChip. 

Trivia

His most viewed video, with more than 4.8 million views, is called "The Best FIFA 15 TOTY Pack Opening Ever!! - 9 x TOTY's!!" He has also posted multiple vlogs documenting events like his trip to New York and his move into a house for Fortnite players. 

Family Life

His name is Josh Larkin. He is from Manchester, England, UK. He has posted pictures with his younger sister on his Instagram. He has been in a relationship with YouTuber Gee Nelson.

Associated With

He did a Q&A video with AnEsonGib in October of 2014.
