Reactions As Sanwo-Olu Turns Church Usher (Video)

The Lagos State Governor, Babajide Sanwo-Olu, assumed the duty of a church usher after service at Harvesters church in the state.

Naija News reports that Sanwo-Olu stormed the church today, March 12, 2023, to worship with the church members.

After the service, the governor stood at the entrance of the church where he exchanged handshakes with members of the church as they left for their homes.

Since last week, Sanwo-Olu has intensified the strategy of his campaign, ahead of the gubernatorial election through different public outings to beg for votes.
