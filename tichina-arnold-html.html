Tichina Arnold - Age, Family, Bio

Portrayed Pamela James on FOX's sitcom Martin as well as Chris's headstrong, assertive mother on Everybody Hates Chris. She later starred on VH1's Daytime Divas and began playing Tina Butler in CBS's The Neighborhood.

Before Fame

She appeared on the TV series One on One.

Trivia

She created a brand of designer headbands that were embellished with Swarovski crystals, which Janet Jackson, Vivica A. Fox and others have worn.

Family Life

She had a daughter named Alijah Kai Haggins with music producer Carvin Haggins. She later married basketball assistant coach DaRico Hines.

Associated With

She was in the comedy Big Momma's House with Martin Lawrence.
