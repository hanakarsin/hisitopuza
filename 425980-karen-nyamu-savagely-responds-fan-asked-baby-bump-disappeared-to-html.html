Karen Nyamu Savagely Responds to Fan Who Asked Where Her Baby Bump Disappeared To

  • Nyamu is known for going 'head to head' with anyone who crosses her way
  • On Sunday, the mother of two shared a photo wearing a long maxi pink dress, but her baby bump wasn't showing, and some of her fans speculated that she isn't pregnant as she claimed
  • One of the controversial politician's followers said: "Hapo hakuna mimba (she is not pregnant)"
  • Nyamu is co-parenting with her two baby daddies DJ Saint Kevin and Samidoh

Controversial politician Karen Nyamu is known for her savage clap backs when rattled.

The mother of two has been busy taking care of her kids and co-parenting with her two baby daddies DJ Saint Kevin and Samidoh.

Nyamu, who revealed that she was three months pregnant with Samidoh's second child in August, on Sunday shared a photo wearing a long maxi pink dress, but her baby bump wasn't showing.
