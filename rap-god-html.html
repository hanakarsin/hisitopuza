Rap God - Artist, Ages, Trivia

Third single from rapper Eminem's eighth studio album, The Marshall Mathers LP 2. The song's lyrics reflect the trials and tribulations he experienced throughout his career. It received a nomination for the Grammy Award for Best Rap Performance.

Trivia

The song was entered into the Guinness World Records as the single containing the most words, clocking in at 1,560 words total. The song's accompanying music video was directed by Rich Lee.
