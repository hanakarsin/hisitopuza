Fani-Kayode Reveals Who Is Behind Insecurity In Zamfara, 9 Other State

Former Minister of Aviation Femi Fani-Kayode has accused a ‘Special Advisor’ of being behind the insecurity rocking Zamfara and 9 other states in Nigeria.

The Peoples Democratic Party (PDP) chieftain made this known in a post on his Twitter handle on Saturday.

Fani-Kayode, who did not name the Advisor, stated that his objective was to destabilise 10 states to ensure President Muhammadu Buhari declares a state of emergency in each one.

He wrote: “The conspiracy to destabilise our nation is coming from within. The culprit is a crooked Special Advisor.

“The objective is to destabilise 10 states including Zamfara & ensure the President declares a state of emergency in each one.

“This man is the enemy within. I will expose him.”

Recall that Fani-Kayode had on Friday vowed to expose the individual stoking the ‘fire of insurgency’ in Zamfara State.

According to the former Minister of Culture and Tourism, the constant insurgent attack in Zamfara is troubling.

Fani-Kayode wrote: “Events unfolding in Zamfara state are deeply troubling.

“My heart goes out to my friend & brother Bello Matawalle and to the people of the state.

“Somebody is purposely stoking the fire of insurgency in Zamfara and orchestrating this crisis.

“We shall expose him. More on this later.”

Naija News reports that Zamfara has been rocked with killings and kidnapping, which led to President Muhammadu Buhari declaring a no-fly zone on the state.

The president also banned mining activities in a bid to put an end to the continuous killing in the state.
