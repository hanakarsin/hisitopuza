Brazil Legend, Cafu Names Liverpool Player That Could Become World Best Soon

Retired Brazilian professional footballer who played as a defender, Marcos Evangelista de Morais, better known as Cafu has hailed English Premier League club player, Trent Alexander-Arnold, for his outstanding performance in Liverpool FC. 

The 50-year-old legend in a recent interview with FIFA said that the England born player who plays as a right-back for the Reds could earn himself a ‘World Best Award’ in the future should he continue with his current energy.

Cafu revealed that he enjoys watching Arnold play. He described the defender as a technically brilliant, adding that being lucky to be in a club as Liverpool would help him even get better in his career.

He said, “Brazilian football legend, Cafu believes Trent Alexander-Arnold has the potential to become one of the world’s best players,”

Cafu added, “He’s a sensational player, a rare talent,” Cafu told FIFA.Com If he keeps playing the way he has been he will be regarded as one of the best players in the world.”

“He has the potential to win The Best FIFA Men’s Player Awards. He’s technically brilliant, he has so much quality. He has a Brazilian style of play.”

“I love watching him play. And playing in such a great team will help him grow.”
