Kirinyaga father reports son to police for growing bhang at home

Nyangati village in Kirinyaga county is under police watch after a 25-year-old man was caught growing marijuana inside his house.

The suspect identified as John Mwangi was reported by his father to the police upon stumbling on a 'small farm' of the illegal substance.

Mwangi's father Richard Muriuki who keeps small herd of cattle had reportedly become suspicious and wondered why the manure often disappears from the cow shed.

“I was wondering who was stealing manure in my compound, only to discover it was my son Mwangi who I reported to the authorities,” he told journalists on Friday, November 29.

At the time incident, Mwangi had relocated to a nearby Kimbimbi shopping cenre where he lived in a rented house.

The detectives from Wang’uru police station uprooted the plants when they visited his abandoned house at his father's compound.

Local area chief Emmacuate Wanjiru Njoka confirmed the suspect was yet to be apprehended.
