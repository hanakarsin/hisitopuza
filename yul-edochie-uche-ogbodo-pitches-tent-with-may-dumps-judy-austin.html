Actress Uche Ogbodo Pitches Tent With May, Dumps Judy Austin

Nollywood actress Uche Ogbodo has made a U-turn after several backslashes following her comment on the post of Yul Edochie after announcing his new family.

Edochie, on 27th April 2022, took to his Instagram page to share photos of his second wife, Judy Austin and their son, Star Munachisom.

Amidst the criticisms, Ogbodo stormed the comment section of his colleague to congratulate him on his new family.

This earned the actress several backlashes from netizens for supporting the infidelity of Yul Edochie to his wife of over 16 years.

However, in a new post shared on her Instagram page, Ogbodo said she would never support polygamy and doesn’t appreciate it, saying she would never indulge in it because of the pains it brings to parties involved.

The actress said that no woman deserves to go through what May Edochie and her children might be going through.

The mother of two girls added that she is actually with May but only supporting Judy Austin to catch a cruise with her haters.

She wrote: “I Uche Ogbodo would never ever support polygamy, I don’t appreciate it and I would never indulge in it. This is because of all the pains it brings to all the parties involved in peace.

“NO WOMAN DESERVES TO GO THROUGH WHAT MAY AND HER CHILDREN MIGHT BE GOING THROUGH RIGHT NOW. MAY IS AN AMAZING WOMAN, I LOVE AND RESPECT HER.

“SO IN ACTUAL SENSE, I AM WITH MAY WITH ALL MY HEART ….. and not what the Lots of you actually think because of my comment on Judy’s Posts. I was only catching cruise with my haters.”
