Which Avenger are you quiz

Which Avenger are you? The Avengers film franchise contains what are arguably some of the best movies in the Marvel Cinematic Universe (MCU). One of the films, Avengers: Endgame, went on to become the highest-grossing movie of all time. The films’ huge fan base has led to fans associating themselves with certain characters in the movies. Which Avengers character are you? Well, take this short Avengers quiz to find out.

Which Avengers Infinity War character are you?

Avengers: Infinity War was the penultimate Avengers movie and performed excellently in the Box Office. Most fans have often taken Avengers quizzes to answer one of the most common questions among the films’ fan base, which Avenger am I? Here is a brief look at the character description of each significant cast member.

Thor Odinson

Known simply as Thor, he is one of the recurring characters in the MCU. He is the eponymous character in the Thor movie trilogy as well as one of the recurring protagonists in the current MCU. He was the only founding member of the Avengers who was not from earth. Thor is best known for his use of the Mjolnir (a mallet-like war-hammer).

Iron Man (Tony Stark)

Tony Stark, also known as Iron Man, had been one of the recurring characters in the MCU up until the end of the infinity saga, which saw him die at the end of Avengers: Endgame. Stark can be described as highly innovative, determined, and with an affinity for taking risks. Here is a look at Iron Man’s main character traits.

Perseverance: Stark once built a flying suit using pieces of scrap metal in a cave. He also developed a new element that helped keep him alive when he was on the verge of dying. His numerous battlefield exploits are often the result of his sheer perseverance.

Bravery: Stark is arguably one of the most basic members of the Avengers. However, this limitation has never deterred him from facing challenges head-on. On one occasion, he faced the powerful Loki without his armor.

Truth-to-self: While it might have been easy for Stark to ride on his father’s fame, he chose to shape his future. He remained true to himself and went on to become greater than his father.

Loyalty: Throughout the Avengers franchise, Stark has always watched out for his friends. This is a testament to the character’s loyalty. His intelligence in handling situations is also one of his greatest virtues, which allows him to accomplish fetes that he would otherwise not.

Captain America

Captain America is perhaps one of the most likable, all-round good characters in the MCU. He is known for his dependability, nobility, loyalty, and honesty. He has an innate ability to lead and harmonize the numerous strengths, personalities, skill sets, and weaknesses in the hugely diverse Avengers team. Captain America often seems like the obvious choice for a team leader due to his ability to ensure that everyone is in line.

Black Widow

The Black Widow, also known as Natasha Romanoff, is one of the female characters in the Avengers franchise. She is a highly-trained operative in the world of martial arts, espionage, and combat. While fully human, her skills often make her appear superhuman. Natasha’s beauty, intelligence, and confidence are some of her defining traits.

She is also a master of deception, often leading her enemies into well-crafted traps. She once said that in her earlier days, she had no moral compass, but since joining S.H.I.E.L.D, she began developing a sense of care for the good of the world.

Hulk (Bruce Banner)

The Hulk was the result of a dose of gamma radiation on scientist Bruce Banner. He is known for his immense strength which is often fuelled by anger. His regeneration, durability, and strength have often proven invaluable to the Avengers team. Bruce Banner possesses unparalleled intellect and expertise in science and in particular the radiation and physics field.

Spiderman (Peter Parker)

Spiderman is best known for his ability to cling to walls and cast spider webs. He also has a precognitive sense often referred to as the spider-sense, which allows for his fantastic balance, agility, and speed. Parker is also well-versed in mechanics, mathematics, engineering, biology, physics, and chemistry. He is known for coming up with innovative devices that complement his powers.

Clint Barton (Hawkeye)

Hawkeye is arguably one of the longest-running yet rarely-mentioned superheroes. He can be described as assertive, introverted, and observant. He is best known for his weapon of choice; a collapsible recurve bow along with a mechanized quiver.

Throughout the franchise, fans have grown to adore some characters and hate others depending on various aspects. While the franchise is now over, the MCU still goes on with some of the characters who were part of the Avengers. So, what Avenger are you?
