Nigeria Produces Six Gold Medals In Wrestling At 2024 All-African Games

published on August 08, 2024
It was an unprecedented evening for Nigerian female wrestling contingents at the 2024 All African Games on Sunday night, March 10, as they ended the night with six gold medals in different categories of the event. In the 13th edition of the All African Games, Blessing Oborodudu grabbed the gold medal in the 68kg category after defeating Cameroonian wrestler, Blandine Nyeh Ngiri, 14-4.
