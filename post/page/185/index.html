published on April 25, 2024
Senate President, Senator Godswill Akpabio, addressed criticisms received in response to a sarcastic comment made during a recent plenary session. During a motion presented by Senator Akintunde Abiodun of Oyo State, Akpabio employed the phrase, leading to public scrutiny and criticism.
