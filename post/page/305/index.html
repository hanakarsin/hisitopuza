Terrorists Release Video Showing Victims Of Kaduna Train Attack

published on February 11, 2024
Bandit terrorists responsible for the attack on Abuja-Kaduna train on the 28th of March has released a video showing victims abducted during the attack. The victims in the video were surrounded by armed men in a forest, while they plead with the government to act. The terrorist in the video were holding rifles and dressed in non-combat gears, with turbans covering their faces.
