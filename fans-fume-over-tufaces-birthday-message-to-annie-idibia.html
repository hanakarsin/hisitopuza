Fans Fume Over Tuface's Birthday Message To Annie Idibia

Legendary Nigerian singer, 2face Idibia has left some fans upset over his birthday message to his wife, Annie Idibia on photo and video sharing app, Instagram.

This comes after the singer sent a birthday message ‘devoid of love emoji and sweetness’, according to fans to the actress and mother of two on her birthday today.

He wrote: “birthday blessings my Queen. Nothing do u” with a photo of Annie shared on his page.

However, this did not sit well with some fans who felt something was amiss in the birthday message sent by the singer.

According to some of the fans, Annie, when celebrating 2face on his special day goes an extra mile to write heart warming messages on social media in celebration of his birthdays.
