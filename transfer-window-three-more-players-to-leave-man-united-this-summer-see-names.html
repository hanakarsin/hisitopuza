Transfer Window: Three More Players To Leave Man United This Summer

The reformation plan in Manchester United has continued as three more players are reportedly set to leave the club this summer.

Naija News reports that the English Premier League club had a disappointing 2021-2022 season. Due to the development, the Red Devils could not qualify for the UEFA Champions League.

The club, however, have been making effort to rebuild the squad ahead of the new season.

Reports by Mirror said the likes of Eric Bailly, Phil Jones and Axel Tuanzebe are up for sale before the transfer window closes this summer.

Naija News understands that their departure will be triggered by the arrival of Lisandro Martinez from Ajax.

Recall that the likes of Paul Pogba, Jesse Lingard, Juan Mata, Edinson Cavani and Nemanja Matic have already departed Old Trafford and set their foot elsewhere at the end of their contracts.

Also, Andreas Pereira is sealing a permanent move to Fulham this week but the Red Devils remain stacked with options at the back.

With Bailly and Jones starting just eight games in all competitions last season, United appear close to signing Martinez, the clear-out at Old Trafford is set to continue.

The Mirror reports that the three defenders deemed surplus to requirements will be moved on.
