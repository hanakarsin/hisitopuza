Nigerians Will Thank The APC Govt In six Months Buhari

President Muhammadu Buhari has said that Nigerians will appreciate the All Progressives Congress–led government in the next six months.

The president said this while receiving members of the Progressive Governors’ Forum led by its Chairman and Governor of Kebbi State, Abubakar Atiku Bagudu, at the State House on Tuesday.

He noted that there would not be any interference in the upcoming election and Nigerians would be allowed to vote who they want.

According to him: “I want Nigerians to know that we respect them and for us to show that we will allow them to vote who they want.

“We all witnessed what happened in Anambra, Ekiti and Osun States. What happened in those states gives me a lot of hope that we are succeeding.”

Buhari went on to state that his administration would not tolerate or allow the use of personal resources in influencing, be it materially, morally or physically in the general election.

He said: “We will not allow anyone to use personal resources or their influence to intimidate other Nigerians. We will not allow intimidation materially, morally or physically. This is the kind of leadership that can emerge and consolidate our nation.

“In six months, Nigerians will appreciate the government of the APC that we are sincere and we respect them.”
