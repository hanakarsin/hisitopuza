Bella Thorne Latest News and Updates

Actress, model, and singer Bella Thorne got her big break on the Disney Channel series Shake It Up, where she starred as CeCe Jones alongside Zendaya. 

Bella has since gone on to star in several other TV series and movies including The DUFF, Famous in Love, and Infamous. Along with acting, Bella has also had success in music, releasing her first single, "Watch Me" in 2011, and more recently, her album "What Do You See Now?"

Like most young Hollywood stars, Bella has also experienced some controversy in her career. In 2020, she launched an OnlyFans account and was accused of scamming her followers after promising "naked" photos that just featured her in lingerie. She went on to claim the money raised would be donated to charitable causes. 

She has also made headlines for her on-again, off-again relationship with YouTuber Tana Mongeau, and for directing her first adult film titled Her & Him.
