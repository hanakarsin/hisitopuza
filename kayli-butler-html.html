Kayli Butler - Age, Family, Bio

YouTube beauty guru who publishes beauty tutorials on her popular channel HeyKayli for her more than 600,000 subscribers. She has also posted a variety of vlogs, DIY videos and craft videos. 

Before Fame

She created her YouTube channel in July of 2011, publishing her first video on September 29, 2011. 

Trivia

She is the wife of Casey Butler and sister-in-law of Shay Carl, the patriarch of the YouTube famous Shaytards family. She has had paid partnerships with companies like Ninja Kitchen and Creative Roots. 

Family Life

She has four children named Brailee, Winston, Layla and Gage.

Associated With

She is the aunt of Avia Butler, AKA PrincessTard.
