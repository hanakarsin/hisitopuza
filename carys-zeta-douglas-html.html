Carys Zeta Douglas - Age, Family, Bio

Only daughter and youngest child of Oscar winning actors Michael Douglas and Catherine Zeta-Jones.

Before Fame

She was born at Valley Hospital in Ridgewood, New Jersey weighing in at 6 lbs and 12 oz.

Trivia

She turned her mother into a dance mom when she performed with the Westchester Dance Academy at the NYC Dance Alliance Foundation's Bright Lights, Shining Stars Gala

Family Life

She has an older brother named Dylan and a half-brother named Cameron.

Associated With

She is the granddaughter of Kirk Douglas and Diana Dill.
