Watch Emotional Moment Jackie B Reunites With Her Son After Her Eviction From BBNaija

Evicted Big Brother Naija Shine Ya Eye ex-housemate, Jackie B on Monday reunited with her son and mum hours after leaving the reality show.

In an emotional video shared on social media, the mother of one Jackie, screamed as she embrace her 10-year-old son tightly who revealed he flew all the way from Abuja to Lagos to surprise her.

Jackie’s B mum was seen hugging and praying for her in the video.

Naija News recalls that Jackie B was evicted from the show on Sunday alongside Jaypaul.

Meanwhile, Big Brother Naija Season 6 housemate, Jackie B in response to Whitemoney‘s love declaration towards her has revealed she wants his kind of man. 

This is coming after fellow housemate, Tega asked her if she wanted to date Whitemoney after he professed his love.

Recall that some weeks back, Whitemoney opened up to Jackie B, where he revealed he was attracted to her and not Queen.

 “I have a lot of love for him. I have a lot of respect for him and he’s definitely someone that I can date, for sure.”Jackie B told Tega in Whitemoney’s presence.

Although, she noted that, she had always seen Whitemoney differently and would have to reprogram her mind to see him as a lover.

“I’m not saying that it’s not possible for me to date him. It can honestly happen but I have to reprogram my mind to start seeing him in that way. But with him, I didn’t even suspect him for one second.

“He’s a mature man, that’s the type of man that I want, a grown-ass man that knows what he wants. That’s my type of person.” she concluded.
