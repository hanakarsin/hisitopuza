Popular Nigerian Skitmaker Declared Missing

Nigerian skitmaker, Ganiyu Kehinde Morufu, popularly known as Ijoba Lande, has been declared missing by his family.

Naija News reports that Ijoba Lande’s wife made the disclosure in a video shared on Instagram.

She revealed her husband left the house without his phone on Sunday night, March 26, after breaking his fast and is yet to return home.

The skitmaker’s wife further pleaded with the general public to visit the nearest police station if they have any information on his whereabouts.

She wrote: “This is to inform the public that IJOBA LANDE left home to an unknown destination on 26th of march around 9:00 pm and up till now we can find him.

“We have reported the case at the nearest police station and with their help they help us to send signal to radio station please if you noticed anthing you can dm his account or @ijesaekun.

“He left his phone at home after breaking his fasting on 26th that was the last time we heard from him”

Ijoba Lande’s colleague, Abdulgafar Ahmad popularly known as Cute Abiola, also took to his Instagram page to raise alarm over the sudden disappearance.

He wrote: “Ah, what is going on? Ijoba Lande is missing. kilonshele ? Any one here knows about his Wer about pls ? ah !”
