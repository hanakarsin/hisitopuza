EverythingApplePro - Age, Family, Bio

Russian-American YouTuber who runs an everything Apple-product-related channel called EverythingApplePro, as well as a secondary Android channel called OfficialPhoneRebel. His videos have included phone drop tests, accessory product tests, Apple news, phone hacks and modifications. 

Before Fame

He created his EverythingApplePro channel in January of 2009. His first videos were not Apple-related, instead relating to the Playstation Portable.

Trivia

He reached the one million subscriber milestone on his primary YouTube channel in November 2015. He has also gained over 480,000 followers for his Apple product photos on his everythingapplepro Instagram page. 

Family Life

His name is Filip Koroy and he is originally from Ukraine, but he has also lived in Vancouver, Washington. He was raised in a Russian household.

Associated With

He and Matthias both post content to YouTube relating to new Apple devices.
