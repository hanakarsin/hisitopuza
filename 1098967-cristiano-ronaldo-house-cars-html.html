Cristiano Ronaldo house and cars

Cristiano Ronaldo is a well-known lover of investment in real estate. So no wonder Cristiano Ronaldo’s house is extremely luxury. In 2009, he bought a hotel in Portugal. A few months later, he found that house in Madrid. And in 2015, the footballer found an apartment in the center of Lisbon and bought it for € 2 million. The last fact that surprised the whole world was a truly royal gift for his agent, Georges Mendes. He presented him a whole Greek island for the wedding.

Cristiano Ronaldo’s house and cars

The main star of Madrid ‘Real’ showed the collection of about 20 cars. Here is the list of the most luxury Cristiano Ronaldo’s cars.

Mercedes-Benz C-Class Sport Coupe 2004

The first car in the collection of Cristiano Ronaldo was acquired in 2004 when the guy was only 19 years old. By that time, footballer had already signed a contract with Manchester United as well as performed for the national team of Portugal and reached the final of the Euro. So he paid 23 thousand euros for his car with no doubts.

Mercedes-Benz C220 CDI 2004

In the same year, after his first season in the English Premier League, Ronaldo bought another Mercedes. The second foreign car in the collection of Cristiano costs 29,000 euros.

BMW M6 2006

The maximum possible speed is 330 km / h. The cost is 94 thousand euros. Ronaldo often used the quick M6 to travel the streets of Manchester in 2008.

Bentley Continental GTC 2007

Cristiano had an expensive luxury car till 2009 when he collected his things and moved to Madrid, where he signed a contract with Real Madrid. The sale of a popular among football players Bentley brought Ronaldo 115 thousand euros while a new car costed him 170 thousand.

2007 Porsche Cayenne

Quite inexpensive (only 80 thousand euros) and, frankly speaking, not the most exclusive exhibit in the fleet of the football player.

Ferrari 599 GTB Fiorano 2008

Ronaldo bought red sport car shortly after winning the Moscow Champions League final, but six months later he crashed it in one of the Manchester tunnels. Without thinking twice, Cristiano bought a similar model instead of the previous one. Both autos of the Portuguese are estimated at 360 thousand pounds sterling.

Audi Q7 de 2008

It is one of the most spacious cars in the collection of Ronaldo. It brought Cristiano to the red carpet before the Golden Ball in 2008.

Ferrari F430 de 2008

In the same year, Ronaldo got a second car of the brand Ferrari. Acceleration to the 100 km / h in 4 seconds and a top speed is 315 km / h - this pleasure costed the Portuguese 217 thousand euros.

Bentley Continental GT Speed de 2009

In 2009, there was the next Bentley with a capacity of 610 hp. Ronaldo paid 159 thousand euros.

Porsche 911 Carrera S Cabriolet de 2009

It was another cabriolet of a football player, which Ronaldo actively used while traveling through the streets of Madrid. It was bought when the football player had already changed the English residence permit and moved from ‘MU’ to ‘Real’.

Aston Martin DB9

Spectacular white Aston Martin appeared in Ronaldo’s auto park when he was a football player of ‘Manchester United’. 135 thousand euros was the needed amount to buy a car, but Cristiano earned this sum in about a week.

Koenigsegg CCX 11, 2009

It is one of the fastest cars, presented in the collection of the football player. The maximum speed of the sports car is 402 km / h, and the cost is 391 thousand euros.

Audi R8 2009, Audi RS6, Audi RS6 Avant 201

Considering the fact that there is a signed contract between Madrid ‘Real’ and Audi, it is not surprising that the personal garage of Cristiano Ronaldo is periodically replenished with cars of the famous German brand.

Maserati GranCabrio 2011

When Ronaldo bought a Maserati, the story of his purchase immediately leaked to the Spanish press: allegedly first Cristiano saw the car during the shootings in advertising, he liked the car so much that on one of the following days the football player got it for more than 150 thousand euros.

Bugatti Veyron

In the case of Bugatti, it all started with a commercial, shot in 2008, in which the star player competed in speed with a jet car. Some time passed - and this iron horse was discovered in the garage of Ronaldo.

Ferrari 599 GTO 2011

This Ferrari was released in limited edition. Of course, one of them immediately was at the disposal of the player of ‘Real’.

Rolls-Royce Phantom Drophead Coupe

It is one of the David Beckham’s favorite cars. Rolls-Royce Phantom was soon noticed in the collection of Cristiano Ronaldo. Its cost is estimated at 410 thousand euros.

Lamborghini Aventador LP 700-4, 2012

On the 27th day of his birth, Ronaldo chose the next four-wheeled gift and bought a black sport car. Cristiano did not come to Madrid very often, and he did not intend to show off an expensive purchase, still the Portuguese man could not hide it from the Spanish paparazzi who managed to take a picture of the player in his garage.

Cristiano Ronaldo’s net worth

Ronaldo's income grew by $ 3 million over the year and amounted about $ 82 million in 2016. $ 53 million of them came from Cristiano Ronaldo’s salary and bonuses at Real, and $ 29 million he got from sponsorship contracts. According to Forbes estimates, Ronaldo is not only the best-paid Football player of the world but also the highest paid athlete in the world in team sports.

For each goal or an assist, Ronaldo received $ 750,000 in 2016.

Cristiano Ronaldo house in Spain

Footballer conducted a video tour of his mansion, located in La Finca in Pozuelo de Alarcon, 10 km from Madrid.

Fans of the athlete were amazed at it. They had the opportunity to see the home of their idol. The mansion for € 6.5 million is located in the elite area. The legend of France Zinedine Zidane also lives nearby.

Ronaldo, who earns € 390.9 thousand a week, invited a videographer to his house, showing the bedroom a living room and a garden. The property of the football player has an area of 798 square meters and is a one-story building. Inside, in addition to the above rooms, there are eight bathrooms and a children's room.

The Portuguese player admits that the main room in the house is the bedroom for him. After all, a healthy dream helps him to recover in order to continue to show good results in the profession.
