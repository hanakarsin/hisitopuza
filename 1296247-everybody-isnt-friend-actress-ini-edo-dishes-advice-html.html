Everybody isnt your friend - Actress Ini Edo dishes out advice

- Nollywood actress, Ini Edo, has taken to social media to share an interesting advice with her fans

- In a post shared to her Instastory, the actress warned people against being trusting in friendships

- Edo further stated that people should know their circle of friends

With the celebrity life comes a lot of wonderful opportunities which often includes going on expensive trips, owning luxurious houses and wearing fancy dresses. However, with the fame often attracts fake friends.

Nollywood actress, Ini Edo, has certainly had her fair share of such friends. She recently took to her Instastory to share a post that warned people about distrustful people who pretend to have one's back.

The post reads: "Everybody isn’t your friend. Just because they hang around you and laugh with you doesn’t mean they’re for you. Just because they say they got got your back, doesn’t mean they won’t stab you in it. People pretend well. Jealousy sometimes doesn’t live far. So know your circle. At the end of the day real situations expose fake people so pay attention”.

Still on Nollywood stars, filmmaker Dolly Unachukwu has taken to social media to share her thoughts on the decay in the Nollywood movie industry. The actress shared her opinion in a lengthy Instagram post.

The actress explained that she has seen it all in the movie industry and fans would not want to know how many of the upcoming and established actors are depressed. According to her, many of them are under pressure to live the good life and meet up with the expected standards. Unachukwu stated that this has made it hard for actors to feel real love for one another.
