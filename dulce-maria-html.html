Dulce Mara - Age, Family, Bio

Mexican singer and actress who has won numerous awards for her work on television as well as for her song "Ya No."

Before Fame

She was cast in the Mexican telenovela Rebelde, which led the the formation of her band, RBD.

Trivia

She was llyena Faustin in the video game Grand Theft Auto IV.

Family Life

She previously dated actor Pablo Lyle and soccer star Memo Ochoa. In November 2019, she married Paco Álvarez. She and Álvarez welcomed their first child in December 2020. She's the great-niece of famed Mexican painter Frida Kahlo.  

Associated With

She starred alongside Alfonso Herrera on the telenovela Rebelde.
