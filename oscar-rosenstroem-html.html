Oscar Rosenstroem - Age, Family, Bio

Popular social media personality who has amassed over 1.5 million fans on his oscarrosenstroem TikTok account. He is known for posting pop lip sync videos as well as short comedy clips.

Before Fame

He started to find success online towards the end of 2015 and into 2016.

Trivia

He has earned over 240,000 followers on Instagram by sharing a range of pictures of himself, often in modeling poses. His Instagram has listed him as a musician and he has also posted multiple photos next to instruments and other musical equipment. 

Family Life

He had a twin sister named Emilie who tragically passed away in January 2018. 

Associated With

He posted a lip-sync video to Cameron Dallas' "She Bad."
