Amelie Bea Smith - Age, Family, Bio

Television and voice actress who is widely recognized for her work as Flora on the 2020 series The Haunting of Bly Manor. She appeared in 9 episodes of the Netflix show. This exposure led to her amassing a following on social media. 

Before Fame

She made her professional acting debut on EastEnders in 2018.

Trivia

As of 2020, she is the voice of the titular character on Peppa Pig. She has amassed over 5 different acting credits throughout her brief career. 

Family Life

She has two sisters, they are from London, England.

Associated With

She appeared alongside Victoria Pedretti and Oliver Jackson Cohen on The Haunting of Bly Manor.
