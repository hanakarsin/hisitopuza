Paris Hilton - Age, Family, Bio

Socialite who achieved massive fame while appearing on the reality series The Simple Life. Also an actress, her first major role came in the 2005 film House of Wax. The role earned her a Teen Choice Award nomination for Breakout Performance. In 2018, she began hosting Viceland's Hollywood Love Story. She has also been the subject of a number of other reality series such as Paris Hilton's My New BFF and Paris in Love. 

Before Fame

She signed with Donald Trump's modeling agency, T Management, at age 19. Some of her early modeling work included being photographed for the magazines Vogue and FHM and walking at New York Fashion Week. 

Trivia

She made a cameo appearance as herself in the 2001 comedy film Zoolander. In 2021, she began hosting a podcast titled This is Paris. 

Family Life

She is Conrad Hilton's great-granddaughter. She had a six month engagement to Paris Latsis in 2005. In 2018, she was engaged briefly to Chris Zylka. She started dating businessman Carter Reum in 2019. The couple got engaged in February 2021 and were married in November 2021. They welcomed their first child together, a boy, via surrogate in January 2023. They welcomed a daughter named London via surrogate in November 2023. 

Associated With

She starred with Nicole Richie on The Simple Life in 2003.
