Hunter King - Age, Family, Bio

Known for playing Summer Newman on the soap opera The Young and the Restless and Adriana Masters on Hollywood Heights. She also appeared in the 2005 film Rebound.

Before Fame

She appeared early in her career in the 2001 science fiction film A.I. Artificial Intelligence.

Trivia

She won a Young Artist Award and a Daytime Emmy Award for her work on The Young and the Restless. She has also had smaller roles in Workaholics, ER, and The Price Is Right.

Family Life

She has three sisters, including actors Joey and Kelli. In August 2018, she got engaged to Nico Svoboda, but the two ended their relationship in 2020.

Associated With

She performed in singer Miley Cyrus' music video "7 Things When She Was Younger."
