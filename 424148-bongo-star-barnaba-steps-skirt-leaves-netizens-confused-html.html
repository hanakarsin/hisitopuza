Bongo Star Barnaba Steps Out in Skirt, Leaves Netizens Confused

  • Barnaba wore a skirt to his video shoot and shared a clip of himself showing off her outfit
  • His fans were confused and wondered if he had gone mad
  • The musician made it clear through his caption that he was working on his latest video and his fashion was inspired by Koffi Olomide

Tanzanian musician Barnaba has gotten his fans talking after sharing a short video of himself wearing a skirt.

The singer on Thursday, August 26, shared a behind the scene video of his new song's video shoot and disclosed his fashion sense was inspired by the song he was about to release.

According to the star, his soon to be released jam was written by fashion-savvy Congolese singer Koffi Olomide.

Video shoot

Barnaba, through his caption, noted that his fashion in the music video will definitely impress Koffi Olomide.

''It is morning with skirts, Mopao on top. You will all understand me I tell you. Guess what is next? Written by my father Koffi Olomide. I want him to come and see me, I will not disappoint him." Barnaba wrote.

Well, he only wore the skirt for the video shoot but fellow Tanzanian celebrities and his fans could not take it lightly.

Quite a number were left confused with many asking him if he had gone mad.

This came quite as a surprise to many of his fans who know him as one of the few celebrities with a clean career and who has never been a fan of publicity stunts.
