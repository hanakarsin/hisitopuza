Monica Granda - Age, Family, Bio

Female bodybuilder known for her humorous approach to working out. Her combination of technique and comedy has earned her 500,000 followers on her Monicaagranda Instagram account. Her monicaagranda TikTok account has 330,000 followers. She uploads gym videos, video game walkthroughs, fan art and reaction videos.

Before Fame

Her first Instagram post from July 9, 2018, features her displaying her biceps with a well-used punching bag.

Trivia

She has published several videos displaying her drawing pad. She loves to sketch fantasy figures and anime characters. She is known for her extreme deadlift ability in bodybuilding competitions. She has posted a photo with famous bodybuilder Cass Martin, whom she credits with being a childhood influence for her.

Family Life

She has posted she spends so many hours at the gym, she considers the support staff her extended family. She was born in the United States.

Associated With

She is a bodybuilder, similar to Iris Kyle and Kelly Ryan.
