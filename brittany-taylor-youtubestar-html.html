Brittany Taylor (YouTube Star) - Age, Family, Bio

Social media personality who is famous for her self-titled YouTube channel. She has gained popularity there for her minimalist lifestyle vlogging, as well as natural health and dieting and simple traveling tips. She has gained over 10,000 followers on her simpleliving13 Instagram account.

Before Fame

She left her office job in 2012 to travel. She began regularly vlogging in November of that same year. 

Trivia

She has earned more than 40,000 YouTube subscribers. 

Family Life

She has a total of five siblings: two blood-related, three step-siblings. She vlogged alongside her longtime partner, Conor McMillen, on their Conor and Brittany YouTube channel. The two had a child together named Ilya before eventually splitting. She later began dating Bridgette Wolleat in 2018.

Associated With

She and Alyse Parker teamed up to do an Acro Yoga Challenge video together.
