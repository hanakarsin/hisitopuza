What Morals Are You Teaching?

Controversial Nollywood actor Uche Maduagwu has criticised his colleague Eniola Badmus over her sultry photo.

Taking to her Instagram page to wish her fans a happy new month, Eniola Badmus shared a photo of herself rocking a blue dress while giving a free show of her thighs.

However, Uche Maduagwu seemed displeased with the photo as he tackled Eniola Badmus questioning the kind of morals she is teaching young people.

Uche Maduagwu, while making a mockery of Eniola Badmus, opined that she wouldn’t have even bothered about wearing any clothes, stating at age 44 the curvy actress ought to know how to dress appropriately.

According to Uche Maduagwu, Eniola Badmus’s outfit is not suitable for the public and may not be addressed by the people reasonably.

He further questioned the kind of morals Eniola Badmus was teaching young people with such a sultry outfit.

He wrote: “Omg Ikunle Abiamo e Gbami is this also fashion Aunty Eniola? Why you no just no cover body Na? At 44 there are some outfit wey elders no suppose dey wear come out, walahi the kind respect wey me get for you and seeing this now O Ma Se, what moral lesson you dey teach our pikins with this dress wey dey show everyone the shape of your Laps?”
