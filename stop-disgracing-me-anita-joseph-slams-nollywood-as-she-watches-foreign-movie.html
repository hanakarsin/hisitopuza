'Stop Disgracing Me' - Anita Joseph Slams Nollywood Producers Over Ghost Scenes

Popular Nollywood actress Anita Joseph has called out Nigerian movie producers for making movies that are not up to par.

The actress uploaded a video via her Instagram page of a movie scene with a ghost.

She called on the producers to see how ghost appears in foreign movies as opposed to the Nigerian version whom she claims are seen in Keke (tricycle), eating pizza, and other funny scenes.

She called on her colleagues to stop disgracing her and make movies with more realistic scenes.

According to her: My Nollywood biko kwa nu I no wan Dey see ghost enter keke even bring pizza for person noooo na Mba ekwerom.”

Meanwhile, Nollywood actress and filmmaker, Halima Abubakar has claimed that producers demand s3x for roles before giving actresses the opportunity to cast a role.

Naija News understands that the actress made the assertion via her verified Twitter account on Saturday after fans inquired about her reasons for her absence in a lot of films produced lately.

According to the filmmaker, she does not have the strength for s3x and is not hungry to lick some asses.
