Sijibomi Ogundele reveals to get his wife the latest Rolls Royce

Sijimobi Ogundele one of the Nigerian youngest billionaire has revealed how he intends to buy his wife the latest Rolls Royce Sweptail which is one of the expensive car ever built at a cost of $13 million.

He wrote: "The most expensive new car ever built. The Rolls Royce Sweptail at a price of nearly $13 million. An automotive equivalent of haute couture, designed and hand-tailored to fit a specific customer... the car i desire to gift my wife."

Well we don't know if Sijimobi is serious about gifting his wife this amazing car but nonetheless she is such a lucky woman.

Sijimobi is the CEO of Sujimoto Construction Company, at 34 he is arguably Nigeria's youngest billionaire, He has developed the luxury real estate market in Nigeria and was featured in the list of Forbes Africa's brave young entrepreneurs.
