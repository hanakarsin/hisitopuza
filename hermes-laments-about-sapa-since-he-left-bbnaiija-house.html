Hermes Laments About 'Sapa' Since He Left BBNaiija House

Former Big Brother Naija ‘Level Up’ Housemate, Hermes Iyele, has taken to social media to lament about ‘sapa’ (a term used in Nigeria to describe a state of being broke).

Naija News reports that the reality TV Star in a video shared on his page complained about how things are currently expensive in the country.

According to Hermes, since he exited the BBNaija show, commodities have skyrocketed and many Nigerians are aware that prices of goods never come down whenever they increase.

Hermes noted that unlike his former lifestyle when he would go to Yaba market to purchase things, he cannot engage in such again because he would make headlines.

The reality star added that ordering things online is also expensive and urged his fans and followers to come up with solutions because things are unnecessarily expensive.

He said: “Omo. Wetin day happen exactly, since I got out of the BBNaija House, e never get wetin no expensive for Naija uno know things when go up no day ever come down. Before you go enter Yaba price some kind thing but na, blogs go carry am, bro papa day.

“Since I came out on the 22nd, I have been trying to buy things, everything dry expensive. Ok make we come order online nkor, bro, everything day expensive. Sapa no fit win o, what is happening. Omo pull up oo I need a solution, celebrity, celebrity, everything is hard.

“Omo, Sapa dey everywhere o. Things are just unnecessarily expensive. How are you guys coping?”
