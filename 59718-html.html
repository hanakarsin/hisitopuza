Dillish and Flavour not dating

BBA 2013 winner Dillish Matthews and Nigerian singer Flavour shared some cozy photos of them together on their instagram pages and this got people insinuating they were dating.

Some even said that's why she broke up with her boyfriend, Stephen.

Flavour and Dillish became friends after she featured in his 'Ikwokrikwo' video last year which he paid her for. She later broke up with her boyfriend and came for a movie in Port Harcourt.

Then she came to Lagos for a visit and had dinner with Flavour. After which she spent the night in his house.

Dillish and Flavour in his compound

Flavour has got a big house in Chevron Estate, Lekki which he bought in November 2013 and so she could have slept in any of the rooms. His PA later took her to the airport. This happened like three weeks ago.

Flavour in front of his house

Well, according to report, there is nothing between the both of them for now as Flavour is said to still be dating Beverly Heels but if something happens in the future, everyone will get to hear about it.
