Musalia Mudavadi's meeting with Kibwana, Karua signals possible political alliance

Amani National Congress (ANC) leader Musalia Mudavadi has reached out to his NARC Kenya colleague Martha Karua and Governor Kivutha Kibwana.

The three leaders confirmed having held a meeting in Nairobi to purposely discuss the future of Kenyan politics as well the economic and social aspects of the country.

State of the nation

Musalia, who first made the revelations via his Twitter handle, did not say much of the deliberation but said talks centred around the state of the nation.

"I met with Martha Karua and Governor Kibwana. We held discussions on the state of the nation and emerging economic, social and political scenarios," he disclosed.

On his part, Kibwana urged all leaders to engage in constructive dialogue as a guarantor of peace towards a potentially divisive 2022 General Election.

Race to succeed Uhuru

The three leaders have declared their intentions to replace President Uhuru Kenyatta come 2022 with Kibwana becoming the latest entrant in the presidential race.

The second time county chief said he had been touched by the plight of Kenyans who implored on him to go for the top seat with the hope of offering alternative leadership.

Through his social media pages in June, he promised to focus on the common citizenry while at the same time addressing their plight should he win the seat.

"I have listened to the voice of Wanjiku asking me to run for presidency in the coming election. I am available, ready and willing to be Wanjiku's president. Will go all the way to the ballot box with Wanjiku in 2022. We must ensure devolution works for our communities," he stated.

The ANC's National Governing Council unanimously also settled on Musalia as its flag bearer for the 2022 contest.

The party's then secretary-general Barrack Muluka confirmed the outfit will only conduct party primaries for the other seats save for that of the presidential contender.

"One of the most critical resolution of the meeting, was the endorsement of Musalia Mudavadi as the party presidential flag bearer for the year 2022. It's now therefore official that the highest organ of the party had given Musalia to go out and begin soliciting for votes and support. There will be no other contenders for the party presidential seat," he said.
