University of Nairobi contacts and campuses

The University of Nairobi (UoN) is a fast-paced higher learning institution offering quality academic programs in line with international standards in training and practice. The main University of Nairobi campus is located at the heart of Nairobi City County in Kenya serving learners from different parts of the world, mostly East and Central Africa. UoN competitively admits thousands of local and international students every academic year for various courses at different levels of study. University of Nairobi contacts are for this reason unveiled to help learners, parents, guardians, staff members, and other stakeholders in accessing essential services with ease.

UoN is a vibrant Kenyan university known for its world-class academic quality; now running separate campuses almost in every major town across the country. We have compiled a comprehensive list of contacts of University of Nairobi to help you access relevant information depending on your immediate needs. UoN contacts provided below are undoubtedly useful and convenient for obtaining critical information from remote, off-campus-areas. Continue reading for physical addresses, telephone, email, fax, box numbers, and social media presence among other vital contacts to connect with the institution.
