P-Lo - Age, Family, Bio

Co-founder of The Heartbreak Gang, AKA The HBK Gang, a hip-hop and music production collective whose members include Iamsu!, Sage The Gemini, and more. He released a single titled, 'Good' ft. Larry June in 2022.

Before Fame

He met Iamsu! in 2005 and would join Iamsu! in the production duo The Invasion. P-Lo was inspired to start making beats after listening to Kanye West's album College Dropout.

Trivia

He has released solo tapes called MBMGC and MBMGC 2. He attended the Day n Vegas festival in 2022.

Family Life

His real name is Paolo Rodriguez. He was born and raised in San Francisco, CA and is of Filipino heritage.

Associated With

He was a producer on Wiz Khalifa's "Bout Me" and Yo Gotti's "Act Right."
