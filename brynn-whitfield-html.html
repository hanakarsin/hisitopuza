Brynn Whitfield - Age, Family, Bio

Reality star who gained fame for being a cast member of season 14 of The Real Housewives of New York City.

Before Fame

In the year 2008, she graduated from Purdue University with a bachelor of arts degree in communications, public relations, and advertising. She also earned a minor in political science. 

Trivia

From 2019 to 2020, she worked as a consulting creative director for Rpr Marketing. In a late 2023 interview, following months of speculation, she confirmed that she would "never" date Shep Rose.

Family Life

Her mother is white and her father is black. She has been romantically linked to Austen Kroll. 

Associated With

She starred alongside Sai De Silva and Ubah Hassan on season 14 of The Real Housewives of New York City.
