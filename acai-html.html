Acai - Age, Family, Bio

Guitar Hero gamer with gameplay videos on YouTube that have been viewed more than 355 million times. He also partnered on Twitch and would earn more than 240,000 followers on the live-streaming platform. He is well known for streaming various Rock Band and Halo games among others.

Before Fame

He began playing Guitar Hero when the first edition of the game was released in 2005. He began streaming on Twitch in late 2016.

Trivia

His original YouTube username was Gamerboy28. His most popular streams were of him playing songs from Guitar Hero III: Legends of Rock. He has uploaded real guitar covers to SoundCloud. He is a father and her shares family content on his guppster Instagram account.

Family Life

His name is Alec Castillo and he is from Maine. He began a long distance relationship with a girl named Olivia. They got married in August 2019, and welcomed their first child together on January 17, 2021.

Associated With

He has posted guitar covers of songs by The Strokes and Arctic Monkeys. He and Olivia have done covers together of Kina Grannis, Fleet Foxes and others.
