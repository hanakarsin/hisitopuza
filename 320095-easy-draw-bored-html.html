50 easy things to draw when bored: a guide for beginners

Drawing is a great way to pass the time, improve your intelligence, and build a new skill. Furthermore, having incredible drawing skills can land both of you well-paying jobs in the future. If you would like to start drawing to amuse yourself, hone your skills, or even as a way of bonding with loved ones, this article recommends easy drawings kids and adults can sketch together.

You can use drawing and sketching as art therapy. It improves creativity and memory and relieves stress. Beginners should draw simple things and gradually open their minds to complex images and paintings.

Easy things to draw with kids

The world's greatest drawing artists began with a pencil, an eraser, and a piece of paper. You will experience similar challenges they encountered but keep pushing. An adult can support a child's talent by buying them drawing materials. You can also draw easy images and leave the painting work to them. Here are fun easy things to draw when bored:

1. Airplane

You can draw an airplane when bored. It will not fly and may not even win an award. Nevertheless, it can make you happy. Overthinking will demoralize you from drawing before you even begin. Take it as a fun game to play with loved ones or alone.

2. Bird

You can draw a bird using a watercolor pencil. Find out the meaning of the bird you want to gift someone as a sketch. For instance, doves symbolize love and peace, cranes symbolize in Asia, owls are considered prophets of doom, and eagles are sacred among the Native Americans.

3. Lion

A lion is the "king of beasts." It signifies courage, royalty, nobility, strength, stateliness, and valor. There are many simple ways of drawing a lion. Add brown or beige colors to the body and a brown mane to the lion's head to make your sketch more appealing. Your drawing may not be perfect, but it is okay if it resembles something close to a lion.

4. Pizza

Pick food when thinking of cool things to draw with kids. Sketch something you are craving as consolation. You will not eat the picture but might feel better emotionally. Drawing a slice of pizza is easy and will only consume a little of your time.

5. A dog and a bone

A dog and a bone are among the easiest things to draw for beginners. You can close your eyes momentarily and imagine a dog with a bone in its mouth. It can be a simple or sophisticated dog and bone drawing with colors and a detailed background.

6. Heart

You probably remember people you love when you feel bored. Draw a heart to keep the memories alive. Write their names on the drawing to add more meaning and value. You can draw a whole heart, a broken one, pieces of a broken heart, or any relatable idea. It is all about creativity.

8. Hot air balloon

You can draw a hot air balloon in your free time. It feels good, especially if you desire to fly in one. Flying in a hot air balloon gives you the ultimate sense of freedom. The ride lets you fight your fears and feel tranquil and comfortable.

9. Rose

Roses symbolize romance, love, beauty, and courage. Add intricate details to your drawn roses, like color schemes to make the drawing pop. A red rose signifies beauty and purity. Yellow roses symbolize friendship, joy, and new beginnings. A thornless one means love at first sight. An orange rose implies fascination, desire, and sensuality.

10. Pineapple

11. Fish

If you need more inspiration, know that a fish represents prosperity, fertility, and luck in most Native American cultures. Drawing a fish can take less than five minutes.
