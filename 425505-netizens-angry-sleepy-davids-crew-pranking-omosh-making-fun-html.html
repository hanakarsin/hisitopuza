Netizens Angry at Sleepy David's Crew for Pranking Omosh: &quot;Making Fun of Him Is Not Ok&quot;

  • Kenyans on Twitter have condemned Sleepy David's crew for airing content showing Omosh ready to sell his house
  • Social media users said the former Tahidi High actor is sick and needs help rather than people using him to build their content
  • Some asked the comedian to come out and apologise for the 'distasteful' prank

Kenyans on social media have criticised Churchill Show comedian Sleepy David's crew for pranking former Tahidi High actor Omosh into selling his house.

Omosh almost sold his house

Earlier in the week, TUKO.co.ke reported pranksters from Komic Gang caught Omosh unawares, ready to let go of his house for KSh 15 million.

When approached at first, the renowned actor was adamant about selling the house, but upon constant persuasions, he was ready to release it.

Omosh had initially vowed not to sell the house built for him by the Kikuyu town-based Sung Timbers in conjunction with other well-wishers.

Netizens' reactions

However, today morning, netizens were irked by the comedian's crew for using the distressed thespian to get content.

Here are some of the reactions by social media users:

"Imagine how Omosh felt after knowing that it was a prank. You are all mean for no reason. Omosh is sick. It's not his liking and making fun of him like this isn't okay."

@heisronny said:

"Man! That sleepy David guy had to throw Omosh under the bus just to market his bottom of the barrel YouTube channel."

@Mejja commented:

"But guys the things you are doing to Omosh is not good. Treat him like one of our own! I'm not happy with you people. Can you all just let him live his life?"
