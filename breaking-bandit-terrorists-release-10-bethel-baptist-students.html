Bandit Terrorists Release 10 Bethel Baptist Students

Bandit terrorists have released ten more students of the Bethel Baptist Secondary School abducted in Kaduna State.

Recall that the students were abducted by the terrorists on July 5 in the Chikun Local Government Area of the state.

The students were released by their abductors after spending about 75 days in the den of their kidnappers.

An official in the school who spoke with Channels TV, disclosed that the students were released after an undisclosed amount of money was paid to the bandits.

He added that the released students have reunited with their parents and had been taken to the hospital for treatment.

Meanwhile, the state police command is yet to react to the development.
