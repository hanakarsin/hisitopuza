Great Turnaround as 18-Year-Old Declared Dead by Doctors Comes Back to Life

- In a twist of fate, Lewis Roberts, jerked back to life after doctors concluded that nothing could be done about saving him

- The teen’s miraculous awakening happened just as plans were already in motion to donate his organs to seven people in need of them

- Lewis' family members were so grateful for having him back as they appreciated people who had shown them much love

A fundraising campaign that was organised for him has since gathered over £1,000 (KSh 128k). The facilitator, Chloe Roberts, revealed that the teen was knocked down by a van in his hometown of Leek.

Stokesentinel gathered that his planned organ donation was going to save seven other people after medical expertise said he could not be saved.

Chloe revealed that Lewis’s health since improved as he is responding well to pain tests and moving his limbs well enough.

She added that the kind of miracle that happened to him is what any family would wish for.

In reaction, Jade, his sister, appreciated the people who donated towards her brother’s healthcare.
