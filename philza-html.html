Philza - Age, Family, Bio

British content creator and video game streamer whose biggest claim to fame is the Ph1LzA YouTube channel. He builds epic Minecraft projects in Hardcore Mode for more than 2.9 million subscribers.

Before Fame

In December 2006, he released his earliest YouTube clip titled Ph1LzA Montage 4 PREVIEW.

Trivia

His first recording to reach 10 million views was 2019's My 5 Years of Minecraft Hardcore (Montage).

Family Life

He has a sister named Aimee. He married his wife Kristin Rosales in 2020.

Associated With

He collaborated with YouTube star JeromeASF on a video in July 2019.
