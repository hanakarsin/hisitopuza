Reno Omokri Speaks On Goodluck Jonathan Dumping PDP

Former Presidential aide, Reno Omokri, on Sunday reacted to the claim that ex-president Goodluck Jonathan has dumped the Peoples Democratic Party.

This is coming after a former Governor of Jigawa State, Sule Lamido, expressed doubt that Jonathan is still a member of the PDP.

Lamido in his statement claimed that Jonathan has not been attending the activities of the party in recent times.

Omokri in reaction to the reports noted that the claims that the former President has dumped PDP as false.

The former aide to Jonathan in a series of posts on his Twitter account called on Nigerians to disregard the report.

He wrote, “The news floating around that former President @GEJonathan has resigned from the @OfficialPDPNig is false. Very false!

“This news is being floated by people who were rejected in their state and went to their slave master’s town to receive dubious ‘honours’. Ignore it.

“Sule Lamido, you have nothing to fear. Former President Jonathan is 64 years old. Throughout his life, he has only been a member of one political party.

“His character is distinct. He is stable. He does not jump from party to party. Relax, Mallam Lamido.”
