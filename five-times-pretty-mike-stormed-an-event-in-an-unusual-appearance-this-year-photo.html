Five Times Pretty Mike Stormed An Event In An Unusual Appearance This Year (Photos)

Controversial socialite, Mike Eze Nwalie Nwogu, better known Pretty Mike, has continued to be the cynosure of all eyes at almost every event he graces due to his uncanny appearance.

No doubt that the Lagos big boy is notorious for attending ceremonies in the weirdest of ways anyone can think of.

In this article, we would be looking at the 5 times, he stormed a event in his usual appearance, thereby bringing style and intrigue into the mix of ceremonies.

Pretty Mike with three ladies donning ‘Animal face’

In Feburauy, Pretty Mike graced a wedding ceremony leading three young animaly masked women.  According to the socialite, the animalistic nature of the human person drived the motive behind the appearance.

Pretty Mike with red-robed individuals

Shortly after, Pretty Mike in March, stormed another birthday party with all red-robe looking individual.

Pretty Mike storms AMVCA with Goat

In May, the Nigerian socialite, Pretty Mike made a statement with his attire at the 2022 Africa Magic Viewers Choice Awards (AMVCA) held in Lagos.

The controversial media personality rocked a black long cape with an accessorized black suit beneath but with his white kid (a baby goat). His attire to the event, stood out amongst others.

Pretty Mike with girls bearing massive eggplant

In October, Pretty Mike attended the funeral ceremony of the mother of the music executive, Don Jazzy with a surprise for other guests with eight women bearing massive eggplant on them.

Pretty Mike with fake Bishops

In August, Pretty Mike of Lagos stormed an event with fake bishops appearing in priestly garb.

According to socialite in the post on his instgram page,  the phony clergy were avoiding camera and declined to comment as someone behind the camera was asking “Are you men of God? Will you like to speak to Nigerians?”
