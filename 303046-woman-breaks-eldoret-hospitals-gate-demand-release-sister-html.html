Woman breaks an Eldoret hospital's gate to demand release of her ailing sister

There was drama in Eldoret after a woman and her mother were forced to break a hospital's gate to gain access where their ailing kin was admitted.

According to Mary Majimbo, she wanted her sister who was admitted at the hospital to be discharged so as to seek medical attention in a different facility.

A report by NTV showed the woman pushed the gate down after she was locked out of the the premises, with officials claiming she wanted to kill the nurses.

While accusing the Alexandria Cancer Centre hospital's management of incompetence, she demanded to be issued with a CCTV footage they claimed showed her threatening to kill the nurses.

Mary and her mother Beatrice Majimbo alleged facility was insensitive to patients by allowing unqualified staff to manage them despite charging exorbitant fees.

"My sister was in pain and her oxygen was finished, she called the nurses to help her but they ignored her because they were eating chicken, it was until 1am when the doctor came and asked why she had not called the nurses," she narrated

The irate woman further claimed at some point her sister was taken to a males ward before being taken back to the females section.

According to Mary and her mother, her sister's condition has worsened ever since she was admitted at the health facility including being unable to walk and developing wounds.

"When she came she was walking, but now she is not walking and she did not have any wound but now she has one which you would not like looking at," said the ailing woman's mother"My question is why they do not want to give us our patient we go somewhere else, we have paid KSh 300,000, brought our title deeds and log book but they do not want to see us," she added.

The hospital director Elias Melly refuted the claims by saying that Mary had personal differences with some individual nurses at the hospital.
