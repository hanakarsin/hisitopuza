Are Amy and Johnny Still Together?

'Love Is Blind' Season 6: Amy and Johnny's Relationship Is Still Going Strong

Amy and Johnny are this season's power couple. They gave an update on their relationship during the reunion.

By Allison DeGrushe

Mar. 13 2024, Updated 10:06 p.m. ET

Season 6 of Love Is Blind is like a wild ride full of drama, love, and did we mention drama?! We've got love triangles that'll make your head spin and couples engaging in nasty arguments.

But amidst the chaos, there's a beacon of love shining through — enter Amy and Johnny! These two lovebirds hit it off immediately, bonding over everything from anime to their deep love for their families. But the burning question on everyone's minds: Are they still going strong?

So, how are Amy and Johnny from 'Love Is Blind'?

During Episode 12, Johnny and Amy both said "I do," making them the only couple to get married on Love Is Blind Season 6. They also appear to be the only couple in Love Is Blind history who said "I do" without having sex before their big day — power to you guys!

After the ceremony, Amy decided to keep her last name and hyphenate it with Johnny's, which makes her Mrs. Amy Cortez-McIntyre now. She expressed her gratitude for finding her soulmate and husband, saying, "I'm so grateful to have found my person - my husband." To celebrate their union, the newlyweds raised a glass of bubbly and exclaimed, "Love is blind!"

During the Season 6 reunion, Amy and Johnny confirmed they are still going.

Johnny and Amy said they wouldn't have taken the leap with anyone else.

In an interview with Elite Daily, Johnny opened up about the behind-the-scenes reality of his and Amy's engagement, revealing that it wasn't quite the fairy-tale moment it appeared to be. In fact, he confessed that the whole experience felt rushed, shaking his nerves as he grappled with the weighty decision.

"I had an issue with the timing aspect of it," he explained to the outlet. "You're supposed to be engaged on day eight or day nine. We were definitely at the point of being boyfriend/girlfriend, but I didn't know what it was going to take to make that next leap into being an engaged couple."

As for Amy, the initial encounter with Johnny was a whirlwind of surprises. Despite admitting that Johnny wasn't her usual type physically, she couldn't deny the connection they shared.

"There were so many things going on in that moment. You're matching the voice to the face, and it's forming a puzzle of some sort," she recalled of first seeing Johnny. "He wasn't the type that I would've typically gone for in the past, but that doesn't change the fact that he's still handsome. Obviously, we had formed a mental and emotional connection, and I wanted to continue exploring that."

Sure, proposal day may have been nerve-wracking, but both Johnny and Amy affirm that they couldn't imagine saying yes to anyone else in the Love Is Blind pods.

"I definitely talked to everyone that was there and we had good conversations," Johnny reflected. "But as far as on that next level of, 'Do I see myself spending the rest of my life with you?' it was really only Amy."

Amy echoed his sentiment, saying, "In the beginning, there were other connections, but I feel like I narrowed it down to Johnny just because of the special connection that we shared."
