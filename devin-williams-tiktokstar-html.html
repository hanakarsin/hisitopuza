Devin Williams (TikTok Star) - Age, Family, Bio

Comedy video creator who became best known for his short-form videos on the TikTok account devin.known. 

Before Fame

He began posting picture content on Instagram in May of 2016.

Trivia

He tagged TheShiggyShow in a video where he dances "The Shiggy." He used the hashtag #DoTheShiggy. On Instagram, he posted a picture of himself hanging upside from a tree branch. In one of his comedy videos, he is dressed in a robe and a shower cap and acting like an old man.

Family Life

He is the brother of Dante' Williams.

Associated With

He collaborates with his brother as well as TheBlackTrunks.
