Shotgun Suge - Age, Family, Bio

Battle rapper also known as Fat Suge who is renowned for his recorded battles with the Ultimate Rap League. He has battled esteemed competitors like Charlie Clips and Math Hoffa. He released a single titled, "Self Made 2" in 2022.

Before Fame

His earliest recorded rap battle was in December 2009 on BlockCityTV.

Trivia

He has a SoundCloud account under the name Fat Suge where he has released many of his original songs.

Family Life

His real name is Jimmie Newsome.

Associated With

One of his most viewed rap battles is against Calicoe with the Ultimate Rap League in December 2010.
