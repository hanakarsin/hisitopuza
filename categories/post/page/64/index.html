Lagos Deputy Governor Advoctes Life Sentence For Rapist

The Deputy Governor of Lagos State, Mrs Adejoke Orelope-Adefulire, has called for life imprisonment, without an option of fine, for convicted rapist. She made the call on Monday while speaking at a news conference to celebrate this year’s International Day of the African Child. A day celebrated yearly since 1991, in honour of the over 700 black high school girls that were slaughtered in Soweto, South Africa, in 1976. The deputy governor who expressed optimism that such sentence, would bring about a maximum reduction in rape cases said: “Despite various instruments and laws enacted to protect the rights of the child, we have continued to witness rape cases and other forms of abuse, including sexual harassment, forced marriages, domestic violence, among others.
