LASU Pioneer Vice-Chancellor, Prof Folabi Olumide Dies At 81

published on March 29, 2024
The pioneer Vice-Chancellor of the Lagos State University (LASU), Professor Folabi Olumide has died at the age of 81. The academic and medical surgeon died on Friday, 8th January after a brief illness, Naija News understands. His death was confirmed in a statement on Friday by the current LASU Vice-Chancellor, Professor Olanrewaju Adigun Fagbohun who prayed God to give family members and other associates the fortitude to bear the loss.
