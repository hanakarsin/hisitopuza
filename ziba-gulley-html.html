Ziba Gulley - Age, Family, Bio

TikTok content creator and social media personality who rose to fame through the use of her xzayx89 account. She shares lifestyle vlogs as well as modest fashion modeling and lip-sync content. She has more than 15 million followers on the platform.

Before Fame

She attended an Indian school in Abu Dhabi during her youth. She went on to obtain a bachelor's degree from MS University, India.

Trivia

She can speak English, Hindi, Urdu & Arabic, in addition to her native Farsi. She once worked for First Gulf Bank, which led to her later landing a job with the Embassy of United States. She worked for TIME Hotels from 2018-2021. Her TikTok channel has become so popular that her videos on the platform regularly receive hundreds of thousands of views.

Family Life

She was born in Abu Dhabi.

Associated With

Both she and fellow content creator Summer Jass were born in Abu Dhabi.
